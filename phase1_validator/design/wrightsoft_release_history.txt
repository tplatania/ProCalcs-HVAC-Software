* In Right-Draw you can easily add separate walls that are different than
  the rest of the walls in the room.
* In Right-Draw you can map items in the Notation Layer to proposal parts.  
  These parts will automatically appear in the Bill of Materials.
* The supply branches that are associated with a particular return branch
  are displayed bold.
* You can easily change which supply branches are associated with a
  return branch.
* You can now have ducts outside the building.
* You can rotate duct registers.
* The wizards have been updated.
* There is a link to EnergyPro available.

This release includes new AHRI equipment data effective as of January 9, 2012.
