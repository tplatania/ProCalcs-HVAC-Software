## What Happened
Created a comprehensive AI training guide covering ACCA Manual J (Load Calculation),
Manual S (Equipment Selection), and Manual D (Duct Design). This document teaches the
ProCalcs validator system the HVAC knowledge it needs to properly evaluate contractor
submissions — not just compare numbers, but understand what those numbers mean.

## Why
Tom identified that the validator needs true ACCA context to properly validate Manual J
reports. Without understanding the standards, the system would just be comparing numbers
blindly. This guide was compiled from ACCA's own published educational materials to
ensure we speak ACCA's language from day one (critical for Phase 3 ACCA approval).
